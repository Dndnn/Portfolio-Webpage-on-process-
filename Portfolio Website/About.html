Sophia Denice Maaño is a student designer pursuing a Multimedia Arts at Lyceum of the Philippines University-Manila. She's passionate about creating functional designs that solve real-world problems. Sophia has experience in layout design, branding, 3d-modeling and user experience design. One of her most notable projects was redesigning a non-profit's website, increasing volunteer and donation rates. In her free time, Sophia enjoys attending design events and staying up-to-date on design trends and technologies.
